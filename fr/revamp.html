What's an Ecobrick?
Take our 30 second, five-slide introduction to ecobricks. This is a great place to start if you're new to ecobricking!
Low Tech. Non-Capital
It's pretty simple. An is a plastic bottle densely packed with used plastic to create a reusable building block that achieves plastic sequestration. To make one, all you need is a stick and a bottle.
Plastic Sequestration?
Ecobricks follow Earth's example. Over the last few billion years, Earth has been steadily capturing and concentration loose carbon out of the atmosphere to put it to good use and green the planet's surface. With ecobricks we're doing the same with our loose plastic.
More than Eco
We all know that plastic pollution is a problem. But ecobricks do more than keep plastic out of the biosphere. They also keep plastic from being recycled or incinerated industrially. Best of all ecobricks can put to use locally to build all sorts of great, truly green things!
Build Green and Great
Ecobricks are put to use in all sorts of practical ways by the global community. In the Philippines we build food forest play parks with them. In the UK, we build earthen round houses. In Indonesia, we make modular furniture for homes and cafes. Be sure to check out the building sections of our site for ideas.
We're here to help
The Global Ecobrick Alliance is an Earth enterprise that supports the global plastic transition movement. Our site maintains best practices and methodologies for using ecobricks. Meanwhile, be sure to use our powerful GoBrik app to log your ecobricks and projects.
