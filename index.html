Welcome to Plastic Transition.
Together we can put our plastic on a safe journey out of the biosphere and out of industry and build our greenest visions.

When we save, segregate and pack plastics into bottles, we can make building blocks that can be reused over and over again. Together we can build green spaces that enrich our community and safely secure plastic out of the biosphere. Together we can transition from plastic to ever greener harmony with Earth’s cycles.

Join us towards plastic transition, sequestration & ocean protection.

Did you know that the best way to keep plastic out of the oceans is to avoid supporting large scale ocean fishing? 46% of ocean plastic is estimated to be discarded fishing nets.

Ecobricks can be used to build modules, furniture, gardens, food forest play parks, and more.

Ecobricking puts plastic on a safe and secure millennial road out of industry and out of the biosphere.

Ecobricking is a way to take personal responsibility for your plastic and begin your plastic transition journey. Through ecobricking we can apply regenerative principles to our plastic and, following the Earth’s example, put it to good and safe use.
